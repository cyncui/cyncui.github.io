Cynthia Cui (pronounced “tsuai”) is an interdisciplinary researcher and creator, interested in how the way human minds allocate attention and the influence it has on consumption of design, as well as forming social relationships.

Cui graduated in 2020 with a BA in Psychology from the University of British Columbia. She is based in Vancouver, BC.
